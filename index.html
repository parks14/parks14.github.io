About Me

I am a software engineer with a background in sales. I worked in a team of sales representatives and managed over 30 overseas wholesale accounts. I started gaining interest in software engineering after using our company’s sales app and seeing how the app was constantly updated based on user feedback.


My growing interest in software engineering eventually led me to enroll and graduate App Academy, a 16 week full stack web development program. At App Academy I spent 80-100 hours a week furthering my skills and knowledge in React, Redux, Ruby on Rails, Javascript, MongoDB, and SQL, among others. Not only did I build strong technical skills, but also strengthened my collaborative and communicative skills.


I have passion for learning about new cultures through travel and food. I love exploring new places and learning new things!
